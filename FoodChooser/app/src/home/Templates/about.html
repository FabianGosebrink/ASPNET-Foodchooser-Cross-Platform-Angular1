Small application for demo purposes.

This application helps you to get suggestions what to cook if you have no idea. You can register and login, manage your food suggestions in lists, publish and unpublish them. If public they will be randomly shown on the startpage also to unregistered users to get some inspiration.
This page is NOT about giving you recipes because the Internet if full with sites to get recipes from. This is just to get inspirations what to bring on your plates.
This application was made for demo purposes and will not send you any newsletters or use your data for anything else.

Made with:

Typescript
AngularJS
Automapper
Bootstrap
WebAPI 2.2
Gulp.js
NPM

Hosted on Azure
